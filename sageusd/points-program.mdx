## Overview

The SAGEUSD Points Program rewards early adopters of the SAGE world computer by distributing points to SAGEUSD holders and LP providers. These points will be converted to $SAGE tokens when the network launches, providing early supporters with a stake in the ecosystem.

<Note>
  Points earned through the SAGEUSD Points Program are not transferable and have no monetary value until they are converted to $SAGE tokens at network launch.
</Note>

## Holding Points

The Holding Points Program rewards users who hold SAGEUSD in their wallets, providing a foundation for the SAGE ecosystem.

<Note>
  To earn holding points, you must maintain a minimum balance of 1,000 SAGEUSD in your wallet. Balances below this threshold will not earn points.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Acquire SAGEUSD">

  <Step title="Automatic Tracking">

## Points Distribution Mechanism

Points are distributed daily to all eligible SAGEUSD holders and LP providers:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Daily Allocation">
    1,000,000 points allocated for SAGEUSD holders and 4,000,000 points for LP providers each day
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proportional Distribution">
    Points distributed proportionally based on your share of total SAGEUSD or LP tokens
  </Step>
  <Step title="Random Timing">
    Distribution occurs at a random time each day to prevent gaming the system
  </Step>
  <Step title="Multiplier Application">
    Both market cap and time-based multipliers are applied cumulatively to your points
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Random Distribution Time

To prevent users from gaming the system by acquiring SAGEUSD just before points distribution and selling right after
(or LPing just before points distribution and removing liquidity right after), the distribution time is randomized each day:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Why Random Timing?">
    Random distribution times prevent market manipulation and ensure that only long-term holders benefit from the points program.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="calendar-day" title="Distribution Examples">
    - Day 1: 1 PM UTC
    - Day 2: 5 PM UTC
    - Day 3: 8 AM UTC
    
    The time changes each day, making it impossible to predict when points will be distributed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user-check" title="Eligibility Snapshot">
    At the random distribution time, a snapshot of all SAGEUSD balances is taken to determine eligibility and allocation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
