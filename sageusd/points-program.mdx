## Overview

The SAGEUSD Points Program rewards early adopters of the SAGE world computer by distributing points to SAGEUSD holders. These points will be converted to $SAGE tokens when the network launches, providing early supporters with a stake in the ecosystem.

<Note>
  Points earned through the SAGEUSD Points Program are not transferable and have no monetary value until they are converted to $SAGE tokens at network launch.
</Note>

## Points Distribution Mechanism

Points are distributed daily to all eligible SAGEUSD holders based on their balance:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Daily Allocation">
    A fixed number of points are allocated for distribution each day
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proportional Distribution">
    Points are distributed proportionally among all eligible SAGEUSD holders
  </Step>
  <Step title="Random Timing">
    Distribution occurs at a random time each day to prevent gaming the system
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rollover">
    Any undistributed points roll over to the next day
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Random Distribution Time

To prevent users from gaming the system by acquiring SAGEUSD just before points distribution and selling right after, the distribution time is randomized each day:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="clock" title="Why Random Timing?">
    Random distribution times prevent market manipulation and ensure that only long-term holders benefit from the points program.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="calendar-day" title="Distribution Examples">
    - Day 1: 1 PM UTC
    - Day 2: 5 PM UTC
    - Day 3: 8 AM UTC
    
    The time changes each day, making it impossible to predict when points will be distributed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user-check" title="Eligibility Snapshot">
    At the random distribution time, a snapshot of all SAGEUSD balances is taken to determine eligibility and allocation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Conversion to $SAGE

<Steps>
  <Step title="Network Launch">
    When the SAGE network launches, the points program will end
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conversion Rate Determination">
    A conversion rate between points and $SAGE tokens will be established
  </Step>
  <Step title="Token Distribution">
    Points will be converted to $SAGE tokens and distributed to holders
  </Step>
  <Step title="No Vesting">
    Tokens will be distributed immediately to holders
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The exact conversion rate between points and $SAGE tokens will be determined closer to network launch. 
  Higher the SAGEUSD market cap, higher the conversion rate, potentially upto 70% of the total token supply.
</Note>

## Referral Program

The SAGEUSD Referral Program allows you to earn additional points by referring new users. This program is designed to accelerate adoption while rewarding community advocates.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Referral Code">
    Find your unique referral code on the SAGEUSD dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share With Network">
    Share your referral code with your network
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permanent Connection">
    When a user uses your code, they're permanently tied to you as their referrer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Bonus Points">
    Both you and your referrals earn bonus points based on the program timeline
  </Step>
</Steps>
