## Yield Generation

One of the unique features of SAGEUSD is its yield generation mechanism, which funds the development of the SAGE world computer:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reserve Allocation">
    USD reserves backing SAGEUSD are allocated to secure, regulated yield-generating instruments
  </Step>
  <Step title="Yield Collection">
    The yield generated from these reserves is collected by the SAGE treasury
  </Step>
  <Step title="Development Funding">
    The collected yield is used to fund the development of the SAGE world computer   
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ecosystem Growth">
    As the SAGEUSD market cap grows, more funding becomes available for development, creating a virtuous cycle
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  This innovative funding mechanism allows SAGE to develop without diluting token value through inflation or relying solely on venture capital.
</Note>
